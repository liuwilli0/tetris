Instructions

There are seven differently shaped tetriminos that fall into a rectangular Matrix. As tetriminos fall, a player may rotate, move, or drop them into their final resting place. They may also hold a tetrimino for later use or use a tetromino stored in the hold slot. If a row or more of cells is completely filled with Blocks, then the line or lines are cleared from the Matrix.

Controls:

Move piece left:
Left arrow key

Move piece right:
Right arrow key

Hard drop (instant drop):
Spacebar

Soft drop (quick drop):
Down arrow key

Rotate piece clockwise:
X or Up arrow key

Z or Ctrl

Rotate piece 180 degrees:
A

Hold piece:
C or Shift

Reset:
F5
